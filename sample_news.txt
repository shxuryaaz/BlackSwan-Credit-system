Tesla Faces Regulatory Investigation Over Safety Concerns

Tesla Inc. is under investigation by federal regulators following reports of safety issues with their autonomous driving systems. The National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA) has opened a formal probe into multiple incidents involving Tesla vehicles.

The investigation comes after several high-profile accidents involving Tesla's Autopilot feature, raising concerns about the company's safety protocols and regulatory compliance. Analysts predict this could impact Tesla's market position and creditworthiness.

The company has stated they are cooperating fully with the investigation and remain confident in their safety systems. However, investors are concerned about potential regulatory fines and the impact on Tesla's reputation in the autonomous vehicle market.
